## How we fund it

- $25,000 base budget per [ERG](/people/employee-resource-groups)
- $10M annual DEI program budget (recruiting, partnerships, sponsorship)
- VPs and SVPs have **DEI as a performance objective**, weighted in their bonus

## Reporting bias or inequity

If you observe or experience bias, harassment, or inequitable treatment:

1. **Manager or skip-level** — fastest resolution path
2. **People Ops Partner** — confidential, see your assigned partner in Workday
3. **DEI Office** — `dei@intuit.example` for systemic concerns
4. **EthicsPoint hotline** — anonymous if you choose: 1-866-INTUIT-1

We do not retaliate against people who report in good faith. Retaliation is its own [Code of Conduct](/culture/code-of-conduct) violation.

## What success looks like

We measure outcomes, not activity:

- Representation at every level matches or exceeds available labor pool
- Engagement and retention parity across demographic groups
- Pay equity 99.5%+ (we're at 100.0% for FY25)
- Promotion velocity parity across demographic groups
- Customer-facing products tested for accessibility before every major launch
