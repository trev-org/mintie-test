## Core principles

We optimize for **customer outcomes**, **safety**, and **velocity** — in that order, when they conflict.

1. **Ship small, ship often.** Median PR size at Intuit is ~150 lines. Median deploy frequency for healthy services is 5+/week.
2. **You build it, you run it.** Service teams own their service end-to-end, including on-call.
3. **Automate the path of least resistance.** If the right thing is hard, fix the path. Don't write a wiki.
4. **Default to observability.** Every service emits structured logs, metrics, and traces. No exceptions.
5. **Security is not optional.** [Secure SDLC](/engineering/security/secure-sdlc) is mandatory; threat modeling kicks in at the design stage.
