Mintie SSO replaces password login with enterprise single sign-on. Sign users in through your IdP, provision them automatically, and tie API access to the same identity.

<Info>
  SSO is gated by the `sso_enabled` flag. Ask your account team to enable it.
</Info>

## What's available

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email-first sign-in" icon="right-to-bracket" href="/setup/sign-in-experience">
    Users enter their email on `/login`; we route them to your IdP.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Identity linking" icon="link" href="/concepts/identity-linking">
    1:1 mapping via `idp_provider` and `idp_user_id`.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Per-org rollout" icon="toggle-on" href="/concepts/feature-flags">
    Gated by the `sso_enabled` LaunchDarkly flag.
  </Card>

  <Card title="WorkOS-backed" icon="layer-group">
    OIDC, SAML, and Directory Sync via WorkOS.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable the flag">
    Your account team flips `sso_enabled` for your org.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your IdP">
    Connect Okta, Google Workspace, or any SAML/OIDC IdP from the admin UI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test, then enforce">
    Verify a test sign-in, then flip "Require SSO" to migrate active sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
