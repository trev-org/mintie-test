---
title: "Google Workspace"
description: "Connect Google Workspace as your identity provider for Mintie"
---

The dedicated Google Workspace connector is available from **Settings → Auth → SSO**. Pick **Google Workspace**, enter your primary domain, and approve the SSO and Directory Sync scopes as a Google super-admin. See [Enable SSO](/setup/enable-sso) for the full walkthrough.

## What the connector offers

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="OAuth admin connect" icon="google">
    A super-admin in your Google Workspace clicks "Connect Google" and
    grants Mintie scoped access. No SAML XML, no client secrets to paste.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Directory sync" icon="people-arrows">
    Users and groups sync from Google to Mintie nightly. New hires appear
    automatically; departures are deactivated within 24 hours.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Connection status" icon="signal">
    Surface in the admin UI: last sync time, user count, scope grant status.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Connect Google Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in as a Google super-admin">
    You must be a super-admin of your Workspace to grant the SSO and
    Directory Sync scopes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your primary domain">
    In Mintie, go to **Settings → Auth → SSO**, choose **Google Workspace**,
    and enter your primary domain (for example, `acme.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the scopes">
    Click **Connect**. Google opens a consent screen — approve the Mintie SSO
    and Directory Sync scopes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a test sign-in">
    Click **Test sign-in** to confirm the round-trip works before you turn on
    [Require SSO](/setup/enable-sso#require-sso-for-your-org).
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Fall back to generic OIDC

If you can't use the dedicated connector, the [generic SAML / OIDC connector](/setup/generic-saml) accepts Google's standard OIDC endpoints:

| Mintie field | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Issuer | `https://accounts.google.com` |
| Authorize URL | `https://accounts.google.com/o/oauth2/v2/auth` |
| Token URL | `https://oauth2.googleapis.com/token` |
| JWKS URL | `https://www.googleapis.com/oauth2/v3/certs` |
| Client ID / Secret | From Google Cloud Console → APIs & Services → Credentials |

<Tip>
  Without the dedicated connector, Google Workspace works for SSO but not for
  auto-provisioning — new hires need to be invited manually until SCIM is
  enabled.
</Tip>
