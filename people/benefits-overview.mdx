## Enrollment

1. Log into **Workday** → **Benefits**.
2. Click **Start Open Enrollment** (or **New Hire Enrollment** if within your first 30 days).
3. Add dependents — birth certificates and marriage licenses required for spouses/children.
4. Submit. You'll get a confirmation email within 24 hours.

## Life events

Got married? Had a kid? Got divorced? You have **31 days** from a qualifying life event to update coverage. After that you wait for open enrollment.

Qualifying events:
- Marriage, divorce, death of spouse
- Birth, adoption, or placement of a child
- Loss of other coverage
- Spouse's open enrollment at their employer

File the event in Workday under **Benefits → Life Event**.

## Open enrollment

Annual window: **early November**. You'll get three reminders. New plan year starts **January 1**.
