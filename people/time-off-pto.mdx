## Flexible PTO

Intuit operates on **flexible PTO** for full-time exempt U.S. employees: there is no fixed accrual or cap. Take what you need, plan it with your manager, and use **Workday → Time Off** to log it.

<Tip>
  Flexible PTO works only when people actually take time off. The unwritten norm is **20+ days per year**. If you've taken fewer than 10 days by October, your manager will check in — and we mean it.
</Tip>

### Approval workflow

| Length            | Approval needed                      |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| 1–4 days          | Manager (informal Slack is fine)     |
| 5–10 consecutive  | Manager (Workday request)            |
| 10+ consecutive   | Manager + Director                   |
| 4+ weeks          | Manager + VP, treated as a sabbatical |

## Year-end shutdown

We close most of the company for **two weeks** at year-end (typically Dec 22 – Jan 2). Tax-season-critical TurboTax engineering and Customer Support remain on rotation — see [Seasonal readiness](/products/turbotax/seasonal-readiness) for who covers what.

## Sick leave

Sick days fall under flexible PTO. Just message your manager — no forms needed. For longer absences (5+ consecutive sick days), file in Workday and contact `disability@intuit.example` to start short-term disability paperwork.

## Bereavement

- **Immediate family** (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 paid days
- **Extended family** (grandparent, in-law, aunt/uncle): 3 paid days
- **Pet**: 1 paid day (yes, really)

Add more as needed under flexible PTO. No documentation required for bereavement.

## Jury duty and civic leave

Paid time off for jury duty, with no cap. For voting, take up to 3 hours of paid time on election day. Volunteer time off is documented separately under [Benefits overview](/people/benefits-overview).
