Welcome — we're glad you're here. This page is your single bookmark for the first 30 days.

<Note>
  If anything is broken, missing, or unclear, ping `#new-hires` on Slack or your onboarding buddy. Don't suffer in silence — we'd rather fix it than have you blocked.
</Note>

## Day one

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your laptop">
    Follow [Equipment setup](/people/equipment-setup). You should have received a shipping notification before your start date. If your laptop didn't arrive, file a [Help Desk ticket](/operations/it/help-desk).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Okta">
    You'll receive an Okta enrollment email at your personal address the day before you start. Set up MFA before logging into anything else. See [SSO with Okta](/operations/it/sso-okta).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join Slack">
    Auto-join channels: `#new-hires`, `#announcements`, `#tech-news`, `#intuit-life`. See [Internal communication channels](/operations/comms/internal-channels).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meet your buddy">
    Your manager will pair you with an onboarding buddy. They are your "stupid question" outlet for the first 60 days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete required training">
    Code of Conduct, Security Awareness, and Privacy Basics — assigned in Workday on day one. **Due within 14 days**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First-week checklist

The full checklist is on [its own page](/people/first-week-checklist). The high-level beats:

- Self-introduction in `#new-hires`
- 1:1s with manager and skip-level
- Read your team's onboarding doc
- Ship a "hello world" change to a real codebase
- Enroll in benefits — see [Benefits overview](/people/benefits-overview). **30-day deadline.**

## 30/60/90 plan template

Your manager will share a 30/60/90 plan within the first week. The template lives in Workday. Common shape:

| Window | Focus                                                 |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| 30     | Learn — codebase, customers, team rituals             |
| 60     | Contribute — own a small project end-to-end           |
| 90     | Lead — drive an initiative, mentor the next new hire  |
