Investing in growth is part of how we power prosperity — yours included.

## Tuition assistance

- **$5,000/year** reimbursed for accredited degree, certificate, or graduate programs related to your role
- Apply through Workday → Learning → **Tuition Assistance**
- Manager and L&D approval required before enrollment
- Reimbursement after course completion with passing grade (B or higher for grad work)

Examples that qualify: MBA, MS in CS, AWS certifications, PMP, CISSP, CPA, design programs.

## Conferences and external training

Each employee gets a **$2,500 annual conference budget** with manager approval. Budget covers:

- Registration (most common)
- Travel and lodging (per [Travel & expense policy](/workplace/travel-expense))
- Workshops and training pre-/post-conference

Submit conference requests in Workday at least 30 days before the event.

## Reading budget

$300/year for books, audiobooks, or educational subscriptions (O'Reilly, MasterClass, A Cloud Guru, etc.). Submit receipts via Workday Expenses with category **Learning**.
