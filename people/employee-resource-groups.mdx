## Joining

ERGs use Slack as the primary channel. Search Slack for `#erg-` and join any group as a member or ally. Some ERGs also have committees (events, mentorship, hiring) — sign up at the annual ERG fair in October.

## Leadership opportunities

ERG leadership counts as **scope and impact** in your performance review. Roles available:

- **Co-chair** (2 per ERG, 2-year terms, ~5 hrs/week)
- **Committee lead** (events, mentorship, comms, hiring, ~2 hrs/week)
- **Member** (no time commitment)

Co-chairs and committee leads receive a stipend and have it documented in their job description.

## Budget

Each ERG receives a base budget of **$25,000/year**, with additional funding available for major events through `dei-budget@intuit.example`. See [DEI](/culture/dei) for company-wide programming.
