Internal mobility is encouraged. Most senior leaders at Intuit have moved at least once internally.

## When you're eligible

- **12 months in role** before applying for a transfer (waiver possible with manager approval)
- **Performance**: Meets or higher in last review
- **Visa-status**: H-1B, L-1, and other visa-holders should consult `legal-immigration@intuit.example` before applying — transfers may require new petitions

## How to apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the role">
    Internal job board: `careers.intuit.example/internal`. Roles are posted there for at least 5 business days before external listings go live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tell your manager">
    **Required**: notify your current manager before applying. If that conversation is hard, your skip-level or People Ops can facilitate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply">
    Submit application through the internal portal. Your Workday data auto-populates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interview">
    Internal interview loops are usually 3–4 rounds, lighter than external. Expect at least one panel with the receiving team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transition">
    If you accept, the standard transition is **4 weeks** (longer for critical roles). Both managers agree on dates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Compensation on transfer

- **Lateral move (same level)**: salary unchanged. Location adjustment if you relocate.
- **Promotion via transfer**: salary adjusted to new level's range. Refresh RSUs may be granted.
- **Voluntary downlevel**: salary may decrease, capped at top of new band.

You **do not** lose unvested RSUs by transferring internally.

## Common transfer paths

| From                | To                          | Notes                                              |
| ------------------- | --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Eng (Consumer)      | Eng (SBSE)                  | Most common. Tax engine ↔ accounting engine.      |
| Eng                 | Product                     | "Tech PM" track. Often a level adjustment.         |
| Customer Support    | Product Operations          | Strong skill match. Workday data is portable.     |
| Sales               | Customer Success            | Common for AEs wanting to go deeper post-sale.    |

## Tour of duty

Some teams offer **6-month tours of duty** for cross-pollination. Talk to your director if you want to explore. Tours don't change your manager-of-record but let you contribute meaningfully to a new domain.
