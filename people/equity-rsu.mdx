## What you got

Most full-time employees receive Restricted Stock Units (RSUs) at hire and during annual refresh. RSUs are grants of Intuit stock (NASDAQ: INTU) that vest over time.

| Grant type            | Typical vesting                              |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| New hire              | 4 years, quarterly, 1-year cliff             |
| Annual refresh        | 4 years, quarterly, no cliff                 |
| Performance refresh   | 3 years, annual, performance-modified        |
| Sign-on (selective)   | 2 years, semi-annual, no cliff               |

Confirm your specific schedule on **E*TRADE** (SSO via Okta).

## FY26 quarterly vesting calendar

Grants vest on the **15th of the month following each fiscal quarter end**. Intuit's fiscal year runs August–July.

| Vest date    | Fiscal quarter |
| ------------ | -------------- |
| Nov 15, 2025 | Q1 FY26        |
| Feb 15, 2026 | Q2 FY26        |
| May 15, 2026 | Q3 FY26        |
| Aug 15, 2026 | Q4 FY26        |

## Tax treatment

RSUs are taxed as **ordinary income** when they vest. Intuit automatically withholds 22% federal (37% if your supplemental wages exceed $1M YTD) plus state and FICA, by selling shares on your behalf — **sell-to-cover**.

<Warning>
  22% federal withholding is **often insufficient** if your marginal rate is higher. Many engineers and managers under-withhold and owe at tax time. Set aside additional cash or adjust extra withholding in Workday. Consult a CPA.
</Warning>

## Selling

You can sell shares through E*TRADE during open windows — typically the 4-6 weeks after each earnings release. Trading windows are announced via `#equity-windows`.

**Insider trading rules apply**. If you have access to material non-public information (MNPI), you must clear trades with `legal-tradedesk@intuit.example` regardless of window status. See [IP policy](/operations/legal/ip-policy).

## ESPP

In addition to RSUs, employees can participate in our Employee Stock Purchase Plan: 15% discount on Intuit stock, lookback to start of offering period, 6-month offering periods. Enroll in Workday during the May or November windows.

## Leaving Intuit

- Unvested RSUs are **forfeited** on your last day.
- Vested-and-released shares are yours forever.
- Vested-but-unreleased: track these — they often release on the next regular vest date.
- ESPP shares purchased: yours.
