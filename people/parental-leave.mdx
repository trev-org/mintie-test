## At a glance

| Role                       | Paid leave                     |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Birth-giving parent        | **16 weeks**, fully paid       |
| Non-birth / secondary parent | **8 weeks**, fully paid      |
| Foster placement           | **8 weeks**, fully paid        |
| Adoption                   | **8 weeks**, fully paid        |

Leaves can be taken intermittently within **12 months of birth/placement**, in blocks of at least 2 weeks.

## How it stacks

- Paid leave runs **concurrent with FMLA** in the U.S. (for eligible employees).
- For birth-giving parents, the first 6-8 weeks are typically classified as short-term disability (medical recovery) and the rest as bonding leave. Both are 100% paid by Intuit — administrative classification only.
- State leave (CA PFL, NJ FLI, NY PFL, etc.) coordinates with our paid leave; you do not get them stacked on top.

## How to request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Notify your manager">
    Verbally or in writing. We recommend giving 30+ days notice when possible, but we don't require it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File in Workday">
    **Workday → Time Off → Leave of Absence → Parental Leave**. Upload an estimated due date or placement letter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Talk to disability">
    `disability@intuit.example` will reach out to confirm dates and answer questions. Your STD claim with The Hartford is filed automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan coverage">
    Work with your manager on a transition plan. Document active projects and assign coverage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## During leave

- **Pay**: continues uninterrupted via Workday — no additional paperwork once leave is approved.
- **Benefits**: medical, dental, vision continue at the same employee cost.
- **RSU vesting**: continues during paid parental leave. If you take additional unpaid leave, vesting may pause.
- **Equipment**: you can keep your laptop. Slack and email access remain on by default.

## Returning

- **Phased return**: optional, work 50/75% schedule for up to 4 weeks.
- **Lactation rooms**: every Intuit office has private rooms — see [Office locations](/workplace/office-locations).
- **Backup childcare**: 20 days/year through Bright Horizons. See [Benefits overview](/people/benefits-overview).
- **No-meeting Mondays**: optional for the first month back.

## What we don't do

- We don't reduce performance ratings due to parental leave.
- We don't claw back signing bonuses if leave overlaps with the bonus retention period.
- We don't require you to "make up" leave time.
