## Medical plans (U.S.)

We offer three medical plans through Anthem and Kaiser. All include preventive care at 100%, mental health parity, and access to Lyra.

| Plan              | Monthly premium (employee) | Deductible | Out-of-pocket max | Best for                              |
| ----------------- | -------------------------- | ---------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Anthem PPO Gold   | $0                         | $500       | $3,000            | Frequent care, specialists            |
| Anthem PPO Silver | $0                         | $1,500     | $5,000            | Most employees                        |
| Anthem HDHP + HSA | $0                         | $3,200     | $6,000            | Healthy folks who want HSA tax breaks |

Family-tier premiums vary by plan. Use the Workday plan comparison tool during enrollment.

### HSA contribution

If you choose the HDHP plan, Intuit contributes:

- **$1,000/year** for individual coverage
- **$2,000/year** for family coverage

You can contribute additional pretax dollars up to the IRS limit ($4,300 individual / $8,550 family in 2026).

## Dental

Two Delta Dental plans:

- **DPPO Standard**: $0 employee premium, 80% basic, 50% major, $1,500 annual max
- **DPPO Plus**: $12/mo, 90% basic, 60% major, $2,500 annual max, ortho coverage included

## Vision

VSP Vision through Anthem. $0 employee premium. Includes:

- Annual exam ($10 copay)
- Frames or contacts up to $200/year
- 20% off second pair

## What's not covered

The most common surprise rejections:

- Cosmetic procedures
- Out-of-network specialists without a referral (PPO will pay reduced)
- Experimental treatments — appeal through Anthem if denied
