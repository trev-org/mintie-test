## Booking travel

Book through **Navan** (formerly TripActions). SSO via Okta.

Navan rules to know:

- Approval is automatic if your trip is **within policy** (price, advance booking, refundable status)
- Out-of-policy trips route to your manager
- Use the **lowest reasonable** option, not the cheapest. Red-eyes and 4-stop itineraries don't help anyone.

## Air travel

| Trip duration | Class allowed                              |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| < 4 hours     | Economy                                    |
| 4–6 hours     | Economy or Premium Economy with approval   |
| > 6 hours     | Business class with VP approval            |
| > 10 hours    | Business class default                     |

Status matching with airlines: not reimbursable, but Navan's status program is portable.

## Hotels

Stay within the **per-diem max** for the city. Navan flags rates above policy. Common caps:

| City             | Per-night max |
| ---------------- | ------------- |
| San Francisco    | $400          |
| New York         | $400          |
| London           | $350          |
| Bangalore        | $200          |
| Most other US    | $250          |
| Most other intl  | $250          |

## Ground transport

- Rideshare: Uber/Lyft, business profile linked to Navan
- Rental cars: midsize or smaller. Insurance via corporate card.
- Public transit: always reimbursable
- Personal car: $0.67/mile (US 2026 IRS rate)

<Warning>
  No first/business-class trains except for international travel >4 hours. The Acela Business is fine. Premium ride-share categories (Uber Black, Lux) are not reimbursable.
</Warning>

## Meals

- **Per-diem**: $75/day domestic, $100/day international
- Group meals: lead can submit one expense covering the team; itemize attendees
- Alcohol: reimbursable only with a customer/partner present. **Never** when traveling with reports under 21.

## Submitting expenses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Snap receipts in real-time">
    Use the Workday Expenses mobile app. Receipts >$25 require an image; >$75 require an itemized receipt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Categorize">
    Pick the right category. Travel, meals, conferences, software all have different GL codes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit weekly">
    Submit at least every 30 days. >60 days old = manager scrutiny.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reimbursement">
    Reimbursements pay out in your next paycheck after manager approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's not reimbursable

- Spouse/partner travel (unless executive-attended and pre-approved)
- Personal entertainment, in-flight wifi for personal use, movies
- Mini-bar, room service alcohol
- Loyalty status fees, lounge memberships
- Traffic tickets and parking violations
- Gifts to coworkers (unless via the recognition program)
