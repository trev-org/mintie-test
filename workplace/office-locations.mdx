## Booking a desk

We use **Robin** for desk booking. Sign in with Okta SSO. Book at least 24 hours ahead in MV, ATL, and BLR — they fill up.

<Tip>
  Engineers in MV: book the **3rd floor "Building 4" pod** for quiet focus. The 1st floor lobby has too much foot traffic.
</Tip>

## Visiting another office

If you're visiting an office outside your home city:

1. Book a desk in Robin **at least 1 week ahead**
2. Email the office lead to coordinate badge access
3. Confirm you're following [Travel & expense policy](/workplace/travel-expense)
4. International travel: notify your manager and Legal — work permit rules apply
