## Getting a badge

- **Issued automatically** at hire. You'll receive instructions to pick it up at security on day one.
- Print badges at MV, ATL, SD, BLR, and TLV; digital-only badges (HID Mobile) at NYC, LON, YTO, and SYD.
- Add the digital badge to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet using the QR code in your provisioning email.

## Access levels

| Level    | Who                                  | What it opens                                   |
| -------- | ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Standard | All employees and FT contractors     | Office floors during business hours             |
| Extended | Approved roles (e.g., on-call eng)   | 24/7 access, server rooms (escorted)            |
| Lab      | Hardware/security teams              | Hardware labs, secure rooms                     |
| Executive | VP+, exec admins                    | Executive floor, board rooms                    |
| Visitor  | Day pass for vendors and guests      | Specific floor, with host escort                |

Request a level change via the [Help Desk](/operations/it/help-desk) — manager approval required.

## Lost or stolen badge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Report immediately">
    Email `physec@intuit.example` and call your office's security desk. We deactivate within 15 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a temporary">
    Visit the security desk. They'll issue a 72-hour temp badge.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a replacement">
    File a Help Desk ticket tagged `badge-replacement`. First replacement is free; subsequent ones are $25 to your office charge code (gentle deterrent).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Visitor badges

Hosts must:

1. Pre-register the visitor in **Envoy** at least 1 hour before arrival
2. Greet the visitor at security and escort them
3. Sign the visitor out when they leave

Don't tailgate strangers through doors. Ever. Even if they look friendly. Especially if they look friendly.

## Tailgating policy

If you see someone tailgating who isn't carrying a badge or being escorted, ask politely if you can help. If you're unsure, walk them to the security desk. We'd rather have an awkward 30 seconds than a bad incident.

## Departures

On your last day, security collects your badge. If you've shipped your laptop after your last day, mail your badge in the same package.
