The Recommendations Engine is what monetizes Credit Karma. Given a member's credit profile and partner offers, it predicts the right product to surface and the conversion likelihood.

## What it predicts

For each (member, offer) pair, we predict:

- **Approval probability** — will the member be approved?
- **Click probability** — will they click?
- **Apply probability** — will they apply?
- **Origination probability** — will the lender originate?
- **Lifetime value** — what's this member worth on this product?

The engine combines these into an **expected value** to rank.

## Inputs

- Member's credit profile (current and historical)
- Member's profile (age, location, employment if provided)
- Behavioral signals (browse and click history)
- Partner's offer terms and underwriting criteria

## Models

We use a layered architecture:

1. **Eligibility filter** — deterministic, rule-based. Excludes offers the member isn't underwriting-eligible for.
2. **Approval likelihood** — gradient-boosted model per partner per product
3. **Conversion stack** — click / apply / originate, modeled per surface
4. **Ranker** — combines + applies business constraints (diversity, fair lending)

We retrain models continuously on labeled data (lender-shared origination outcomes for matched members).

## Fairness and Reg B

The engine is regulated under Reg B (no disparate impact on protected classes). Controls:

- Protected-class data (race, gender, etc.) **never** enters the model
- Disparate impact analysis runs nightly; alerts route to compliance
- Independent annual audit
- Documented model risk reviews per [ML platform](/engineering/services/ml-platform) governance

## Surfaces

Recommendations appear in:

- Member home dashboard
- Product-specific feeds (Cards, Loans, Auto, Insurance)
- Email and push (with consent)
- In-app onboarding
- Partner-side widgets (white-labeled)

## A/B testing

Every change to ranking is A/B tested. Online metrics: CTR, apply, origination, satisfaction. Guardrails: disparate impact, member trust scores, partner relationships.
