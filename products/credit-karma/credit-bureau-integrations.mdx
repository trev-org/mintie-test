Credit Karma's product depends on credit data from the three major U.S. bureaus and equivalents abroad. We pull credit reports under the **member's authorization** and **soft-pull only** (no impact on credit score).

## Bureaus we use

| Region           | Bureaus                                     |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| United States    | TransUnion (primary), Equifax (secondary)   |
| Canada           | TransUnion Canada, Equifax Canada           |
| United Kingdom   | TransUnion UK, Equifax UK                   |

Note: we do **not** pull from Experian for U.S. consumer reports.

## Pulling reports

Members authorize a soft pull at signup. We re-pull weekly for active members, on-demand if they request a refresh. The pull is identified as Credit Karma's reseller account; no hard inquiry is created.

The reseller relationship requires:

- **Permissible purpose** under FCRA (member-authorized)
- **Identity verification** before the first pull (KBA + document)
- **Annual audits** by the bureau

## Score model

We surface VantageScore 3.0 (and 4.0 in 2026), refreshed weekly. We do **not** display FICO scores — those require a different data agreement.

## Data shape

The bureau returns:

- Personal information (name variants, addresses, SSN)
- Tradelines (each credit account)
- Public records (collections, judgments, bankruptcies)
- Inquiries (hard and soft)
- Score and score factors

We model this internally as the **Credit Profile** schema, normalized across bureaus.

## Disputes

Members can dispute items directly through Credit Karma:

- We forward the dispute to the bureau
- Bureau investigates with the data furnisher (the lender)
- Resolution typically 30 days
- Credit Profile updates on next refresh

## Privacy and security

Credit reports are highly sensitive — equivalent to `pii_critical` in our [Data classification](/engineering/security/data-classification). Storage controls:

- Encrypted at rest with member-derived KMS keys
- Tokenized SSNs (the bureau's match keys are tokens to us too)
- Access audit logs retained 7 years
- Member data exportable and deletable per CCPA / GDPR
