We issue **AmEx corporate cards** to most full-time employees who travel or have approved spend authority.

## Eligibility

- All FT employees who travel for Intuit
- Engineers and PMs who attend conferences
- Sales / GTM
- Customer-facing roles
- Executives and VPs

Contractors and interns generally do not receive corporate cards. Exceptions through manager + finance.

## Applying

Apply through Workday → Finance → **Corporate Card Application**. Manager approval. Card arrives in 5-7 business days.

## Personal liability vs. corporate liability

Intuit holds the corporate liability — you're not personally on the hook for legitimate Intuit expenses. **However**:

- Personal use makes you personally liable
- Mixing personal and Intuit charges on the card is a Code of Conduct violation
- Persistent late expense submission can result in card suspension and personal-liability conversion of unsubmitted balance

## Acceptable use

- Travel: airfare, hotels, ground transport, business meals (per [Travel & expense](/workplace/travel-expense))
- Conferences and training (per pre-approval, per [Learning & development](/people/learning-development))
- Customer / partner entertainment (with valid business purpose)
- Software subscriptions (one-time only; recurring needs to go through procurement)
- Office supplies (small)

## Not acceptable

- Personal purchases of any kind
- Cash advances (the card disables this)
- Splitting personal/business charges (use a personal card for personal items)
- Loans / family bills / etc.
- Gambling, adult content (the card auto-blocks)

## Receipts and reconciliation

Charges sync into Workday Expenses automatically. You **must**:

1. Categorize each charge within 30 days
2. Upload receipt for charges > $25
3. Submit for approval

Unsubmitted charges past 60 days trigger reminders; past 90 days, your card is suspended.

## Lost or stolen

- Call AmEx fraud line on the back of the card immediately
- File a [Help Desk ticket](/operations/it/help-desk) tagged `corp-card`
- Replacement card in 3-5 business days

## Limits

Default limits:

- $25,000 / month for most employees
- Higher for travel-heavy roles
- Lower for new hires (first 90 days, $5,000)

Need a temporary increase for a specific event? Request via Workday → Finance → **Card Limit Increase**.
