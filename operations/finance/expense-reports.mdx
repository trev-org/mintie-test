We use **Workday Expenses** for all reimbursable spend. Mobile app captures receipts; desktop submits.

## Filing flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Snap the receipt">
    Use the Workday Expenses mobile app at the time of purchase. OCR auto-extracts vendor, amount, date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Categorize">
    Pick the right category. Common ones: Travel - Air, Travel - Hotel, Meals - Customer, Meals - Solo, Conferences, Software, Office Supplies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allocate">
    Charge to the right project or cost center. Defaults to your home cost center.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submit weekly. > 60 days old gets manager scrutiny; > 90 days may be denied.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reimbursement">
    Direct deposit on next paycheck after manager approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Receipt rules

| Spend amount | Receipt requirement                                    |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| < $25        | Not required (but recommended)                          |
| $25 - $75    | Receipt required (image OK)                            |
| > $75        | Itemized receipt required                              |
| > $250       | Itemized receipt + business purpose narrative          |
| > $1,000     | Pre-approval required for non-travel                   |

## What's reimbursable

See [Travel & expense](/workplace/travel-expense) for travel-specific rules. General reimbursables:

- Software / tooling for work (with manager pre-approval if not in [Software catalog](/operations/it/software-catalog))
- Books and learning materials (up to $300/yr — see [Learning & development](/people/learning-development))
- Conference registration and travel ($2,500/yr default budget)
- Office supplies (pens, sticky notes, etc.)
- Commute parking when visiting non-home offices

## What's not reimbursable

- Anything personal (clothing, gifts to family, etc.)
- Spousal travel without pre-approval
- Speeding tickets, parking violations
- Lost item replacements
- Meals during normal commute
- Alcohol unless meeting customer/partner

## Approvals

| Spend tier            | Approver                                |
| --------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Standard              | Manager                                 |
| > $1,000              | Manager + Director                      |
| > $5,000              | Director + VP                           |
| > $25,000 single item | Pre-approval via [Procurement](/operations/finance/procurement) required |

## Late submissions

- Submitted within 30 days of expense: standard
- 30-60 days: requires brief explanation
- 60-90 days: manager + finance review
- > 90 days: typically denied, exceptions only with VP signoff

The clock starts on the expense date, not the trip end date.
