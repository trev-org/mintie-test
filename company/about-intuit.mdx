## Where we operate

We have a meaningful presence in the U.S., Canada, the U.K., India, Australia, and Israel, with engineering hubs in Mountain View, San Diego, Atlanta, Plano, Toronto, London, Bangalore, and Tel Aviv. See [Office locations](/workplace/office-locations) for site contacts and amenities.

## How we make money

Revenue comes primarily from four product platforms — see [Business segments](/company/business-segments) for the detailed breakdown:

1. **Small Business & Self-Employed** — QuickBooks, Mailchimp, Payroll, Payments
2. **Consumer** — TurboTax (DIY and Live Assisted)
3. **Credit Karma** — Personal finance, credit, lending offers
4. **ProTax** — Tools for accounting professionals (Lacerte, ProConnect, ProSeries)

## Strategic priorities

Our company-wide priorities for FY26 are:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Done-for-you AI">
    Embed Genos-powered AI agents across every product so customers get more done with fewer clicks. See [Genos AI](/products/cross/genos-ai).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect ecosystem">
    Make the platform feel like one product, not four. Cross-product data sharing is the wedge. See [Data sharing](/products/cross/data-sharing).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Speed of innovation">
    Reduce time from idea to GA. Engineering productivity is a board-level metric.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Global expansion">
    Grow Mailchimp and QuickBooks Online internationally, especially in the U.K. and AU.
  </Step>
</Steps>
