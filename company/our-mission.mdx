> **Power prosperity around the world.**

This is the lens we use to make every decision — from which products to build, to who we hire, to how we measure success.

## What "prosperity" means to us

Prosperity is more than wealth. It's the ability to thrive financially: make confident decisions, save for what matters, run a successful business, and live without constant financial anxiety.

For each customer segment, prosperity looks different:

| Segment             | What prosperity looks like                                                  |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Consumers           | Maximum tax refund, healthier credit, smarter borrowing, better savings     |
| Small businesses    | Cash flow visibility, fewer hours on the books, easier hiring and payroll   |
| Mid-market          | Connected finance, accounting, and HR systems with real-time insight        |
| Accountants         | More clients served per hour, fewer manual reconciliations, happier clients |

## Our true north metrics

We track three customer-outcome metrics company-wide. These aren't vanity metrics — they show up in board decks, OKRs, and town halls.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Customer benefit" icon="heart">
    Total dollars saved, earned, or refunded across our products. Targeted to grow 20%+ YoY.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active customer growth" icon="user-plus">
    Net new active customers each quarter, with cross-product activation as a bonus multiplier.
  </Card>
  <Card title="NPS" icon="smile">
    Survey-based loyalty score per product. We publish results internally every month.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
